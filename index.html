About Monica Metcalf

Animator

Studied a veriaty of Animation styles in college career from 3D to Pixelation. Monica's personal favorite animation form is tradiational hand drawn.

Web Developer

Pasionet about web development and creating user friendly interfaces. My goal with my websites is to not sacrifice usabilty for aesthetics, and vise versa. Good desgin is about a seamless marriage between the two.

Gaphic Designer

Taking the themes of a company and what it stands for, and creating a visual represenation that displays them is always a welcom puzzle for Monica.

Tea Pixlation

A series of staged images stiched together to make a moving image

This is a pixlation animation I made for my intro to animation class at NYU. With this project I really wanted to take advantages of the medium so I came up with this. It's staring my lovely cousin who very kindly let me dump tea onto her shirts.

  • Date: June 2017
  • NYU Summer Animation class

Bouncing Fish

My spin on the classic bouncing ball.

This project has helped me realize a love for puffer fish. They have such beautiful colors and their ability to inflate is pretty great. This made it easy for me to create a simple but loveable character.

  • Date: June 2017
  • NYU Summer Animation class
  • Category: Graphic Design

Daydreaming Boil

An example of a boil

For this project I hand drew the background and the frames of the girl then scanned them in and colored them using photoshop. We then combine the new background and character in Aftereffects to make the final prduct.

  • Date: June 2017
  • NYU Summer Animation class

Bear and Girl

My final project for my NYU animation class

This was a fun project to make though was the longest one I had made at the time. It is still a little rough in places (I plan to clean them up) but overall I really enjoy the way the linework annd the backgrounds work together. I hand painted the backgrounds with watercolors and scanned them in, then animated the girl and bear with photoshop. The final scenes I put together in Premiere.

  • Date: June 2017
  • NYU Summer Animation class
